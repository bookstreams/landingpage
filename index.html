WHAT IS BOOKSTREAMS

A different way of reading books, based on the idea of giving each book a unique story and promoting cultural socializing through book sharing.
All you have to do is to use the bookstreams app to add your books to the cloud library.
Thanks to geolocation you can always find the books around you and choose your next read.

WHY BOOKSTREAMS

Cloud Library
